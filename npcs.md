## Wellan Cray
**Role:** Cloth merchant
**Appearance:** Around 50 years old, gray streaking dark hair and beard, weathered from travel, bruised temple from bandit attack
**Personality:** Grateful, talkative (nervous energy), family man, practical merchant, experienced traveler
**Relationship:** Ally - Tamwin saved his life
**First Met:** Day 1, forest clearing (rescued from bandits)

### What Tamwin Knows
- Cloth merchant working trade route, based in Millstone
- Wife named Marda, two daughters waiting at home
- Was ambushed by three bandits on Old Road, beaten and robbed
- Tamwin killed one bandit, drove off the other two, saved Wellan's life and coin
- Very grateful - paid for Tamwin's room and board at Sack and Bushel
- Says roads have gotten worse this past year (bandits, hedge knights, worse things)
- Mentioned village headman looking for militia volunteers
- Has been traveling merchant routes for ~20 years
- Owes Tamwin a life debt - "my door's always open"

## Harren Blackwood
**Role:** Village headman of Millstone
**Appearance:** 40s, broad-shouldered, iron-grey hair, close-cropped beard, well-made but simple tunic
**Personality:** Blunt, pragmatic, exhausted, direct, no-nonsense, respects competence
**Relationship:** Employer - hired Tamwin to solve murders
**First Met:** Day 2 morning, his house (blue shutters, east side of square)

### What Tamwin Knows
- Village headman, lives in largest house in square (blue shutters)
- Has a son named Denn (~18, dark-haired, stubborn) - they argue
- Desperate to solve murder crisis before village tears itself apart
- Hasn't slept properly in days
- **Hired Tamwin for 30 gp** to find and stop whatever's killing villagers
- Believes it's feral dogs, not supernatural
- Sent hunting parties but tracks peter out in northern forest
- Trusts Elda Farrow is innocent despite village rumors
- Bought Tamwin's light crossbow for 10 gp for militia use
- Respects methodical investigation approach
- Said "prior planning" would be smart
- Gave Tamwin information on survivors and attack locations
- Wants Tamwin to return with a plan before taking action

### Deaths/Attacks Information from Harren:
- **Joss Mender** (shepherd, 60): First death 2 weeks ago, found near north pastures, throat torn out, face mauled
- **Petyr** (Mara's boy, 16): Second death 9 days ago, worked father's kennels, found behind mill, same wounds
- **Old Cass** (washerwoman): Third death 4 days ago, killed in her own yard near south gate, same pattern
- **Bess** (miller's wife): Survivor, wounded arm/shoulder, fought off with poker, said it was dogs/wolves, dark and fast
- **Tym** (tanner's son): Survivor, mauled outside smithy, father Aldric drove attackers off with torch
