## Ma Badgerfoot
**Role:** Mother, homemaker
**Appearance:** Aging halfling woman with warm eyes and work-worn hands, graying hair in a practical bun
**Personality:** Caring, practical, protective of her son
**Relationship:** Tamwin's mother
**Last Seen:** Day 1 morning, Burlap (Bagshott Row)

### What Tamwin Knows
- Ma is getting on in years but still spry
- She packed him enough provisions to "feed a small army"
- Proud of him but worried about him leaving
- Lives with Pa under Bagshott Row in Burlap

## Pa Badgerfoot
**Role:** Father, retired worker
**Appearance:** Not particularly tall for a halfling (source of old jokes about Tamwin's height), weathered face
**Personality:** Stoic, supportive, respects Tamwin's choices
**Relationship:** Tamwin's father
**Last Seen:** Day 1 morning, Burlap (Bagshott Row)

### What Tamwin Knows

- Aging alongside Ma

## Wellan Cray
**Role:** Cloth merchant
**Appearance:** Around 50 years old, gray streaking dark hair and beard, weathered from travel, bruised temple from bandit attack
**Personality:** Grateful, talkative (nervous energy), family man, practical merchant, experienced traveler
**Relationship:** Ally - Tamwin saved his life
**First Met:** Day 1, forest clearing (rescued from bandits)

### What Tamwin Knows
- Cloth merchant working trade route, based in Millstone
- Wife named Marda, two daughters waiting at home
- Was ambushed by three bandits on Old Road, beaten and robbed
- Tamwin killed one bandit, drove off the other two, saved Wellan's life and coin
- Very grateful - paid for Tamwin's room and board at Sack and Bushel
- Says roads have gotten worse this past year (bandits, hedge knights, worse things)
- Mentioned village headman looking for militia volunteers
- Has been traveling merchant routes for ~20 years
- Owes Tamwin a life debt - "my door's always open"

## Joram
**Role:** Innkeeper, Sack and Bushel inn
**Appearance:** Man in his forties, thin and weathered, practiced efficiency
**Personality:** Professional, respectful once he understood what happened, hospitable
**Relationship:** Friendly - Wellan called in favor for Tamwin's room
**First Met:** Day 1 evening, Millstone (Sack and Bushel inn)

### What Tamwin Knows
- Runs the Sack and Bushel inn in Millstone (only inn in village)
- Friend/contact of Wellan Cray (merchant)
- Wife is a skilled healer - treated Tamwin's shoulder wound
- Offered Tamwin free drinks for saving Wellan
- Wellan arranged room and board as payment
