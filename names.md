- **Silverwood** — Elven domain
- **Dalereach** — Human territories
- **Aeltharin Vale** — Site of the battle between Dalereach and Silverwood forces (~20 years ago)
- **Thornbury** — Human settlement, three days west of the Silverwood on the roads
